# Energy-farm-optimized-installation
Capacity factors of wind farm installations and optimization based on energy demands in Norway. Data provided by SSB and the course MAT1020: Mathematics and Sustainable Management, Spring 2025, University of Oslo. 
The files and codes are for the mandatory assignment for this course.

kapasitetsfaktorer_vind_onshore_daily_2014-20.csv
